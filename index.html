Health and Wellness Services

Here at Miami, "wellness" is redefined. Everything from coffee overdoses to existential crises (often resulting from excessive "studying") can be handled at our health clinic. Furthermore, thanks to the strategically placed "hydration stations" across campus, no student is ever too far from a quick pick-me-up.

A Supportive Community

Our university authorities genuinely care about the welfare of our student body. They frequently drive about campus in golf carts to distribute "Good Conduct and Better Choices" brochures. We take pride in our proactive approach to student safety, keeping a closed eye on things to make sure everyone is comfortable.

Academics

At Miami University, where academics is our middle name! Because our courses are so difficult, students frequently require a little "liquid courage" in order to persevere through those late-night study sessions. We take great satisfaction in offering a challenging curriculum that will force you to reevaluate your decisions in life. In fact, our students have become experts at striking a balance between beer pong and textbooks. Our instructors are well known for their commitment to keeping you alert at all times. You'll soon discover why Miami students are masters at coming up with original ways to relax—between the demanding coursework and never-ending projects. Nothing, after all, says "higher education" quite like an espresso shot followed by a stronger shot. Graduating from Miami is a badge of honor that demonstrates your talent for study and your ability to function on little sleep and lots of caffeine. We take great pride in the fact that our alumni are well-rounded people who can navigate boardrooms and bars with equal grace. Join us at Miami University if you're prepared for an intense and life-changing academic experience. Here, we really believe that you should work hard and play even harder. After all, what use is a college degree if you can't enjoy yourself a little while studying?
