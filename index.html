The topic for my store is computer parts, I went for a pretty generic online store layout. The buttons are all responsive, and when you hover over the item, they do a little animation, and then when you click on the item, it opens up to display more details about what the item is used for. This final design follows my sketches and wireframes almost identically, except for the image layout. After working with it, I like the look of these images more, rather than the small image to the left of the text.
